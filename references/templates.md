# Writing Templates, Formulas & Examples

## POST TEMPLATES

### Template 1: Teaching Post
TRAILER:
[Counterintuitive statement -- 1 line]
[Who this affects -- 1 line]
[What you're about to teach -- 1 line]
MEAT:
Here's exactly how [process] works:
1. [Step with specific detail]
2. [Step with specific detail]
3. [Step with specific detail]
Result: [specific outcome with number]
CTC: What's your current approach to [topic]?

### Template 2: Story Post (PAIPS)
TRAILER:
[The outcome -- start at the end]
[The stakes]
[The hook]
MEAT (PAIPS):
Pain -> Agitate -> Intrigue -> Positive Future -> Solution
CTC: Have you ever faced [similar situation]?

### Template 3: Contrarian Post
TRAILER:
[Challenge conventional wisdom]
[Why this belief hurts people]
[The alternative]
MEAT:
Everyone tells you to [common advice]. Here's why that's wrong:
[Reason 1] [Reason 2] [Reason 3]
The truth: [Your contrarian position]
CTC: Do you agree -- or am I missing something?

### Template 4: Listicle Post
TRAILER:
[Number] things [audience] wishes they knew about [topic]:
(Most people only know the first 2)
MEAT: 1-7 items, 1-2 lines each
CTC: Which surprised you most?

### Template 5: Teardown Post
TRAILER:
I [analyzed/built/broke] [specific thing].
Here's exactly what I found:
[Most surprising result]
MEAT: Setup -> What I did -> What happened -> What I learned
CTC: Have you tried [the approach]?

## OUTREACH TEMPLATES

### Connection Request (300 chars)
Saw your post on [specific topic] -- [specific observation].
Working on something similar in [your niche]. Would love to connect.

### First DM
Hey [Name], [specific observation about their work].
Thought of you when I came across [tool/insight] -- [why relevant to them].
[Open question]

### Follow-up DM
Hey [Name], circling back --
[New unrelated insight. 1-2 sentences. Zero ask.]

## NEWSLETTER TEMPLATE
SUBJECT LINE: [Test before sending]
OPENING: Pattern interrupt -> why this matters -> what they get
SECTION 1: THE PROBLEM
SECTION 2: WHY COMMON SOLUTION FAILS
SECTION 3: YOUR SOLUTION (4-5 tips)
SECTION 4: ACTION STEP -- one thing to do this week
CLOSING: 1-line recap + soft product mention

### Pre-CTA (Friday)
This week's newsletter drops tomorrow.
Topic: [Topic]

Not subscribed: [link in comments]

### Post-CTA (Sunday)
Yesterday's newsletter went out on [topic].
Key takeaway: [Most valuable insight]
Missed it? Read here: [link in comments]

## VISUAL GUIDE
Teaching/Steps -> Carousel PDF
Comparison -> Side-by-side infographic
Stats -> Single bold stat graphic (4x5)
Story -> No visual needed
Teardown -> Screenshot + annotation
